About iCheckPass

iCheckPass is a QR-based attendance website application developed for Immaculate Conception Polytechnic, Meycauyan. It was designed to make attendance monitoring faster, more secure, and more efficient by replacing manual checking with QR code scanning. The system minimizes errors, prevents proxy attendance, and automates the process of logging attendance in real-time.

The system aims to promote digital transformation in school operations‚Äîsaving time, improving record accuracy, and supporting transparency between students and teachers. It serves as both a productivity tool and a step toward smarter campus management.
